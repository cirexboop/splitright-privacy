Privacy Policy

SplitRight · Last updated March 2026

SplitRight ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the SplitRight app handles your information.

Information We Collect

SplitRight does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All bill calculations are performed locally on your device and are never sent to any server.

Advertising

SplitRight displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain device information and identifiers to serve relevant advertisements. This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with their privacy policy. You can review Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

You can permanently remove all ads from SplitRight by purchasing the "Remove Ads" in-app purchase.

In-App Purchases

SplitRight offers optional in-app purchases including Remove Ads, Theme Pack, and tip options. All purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect, store, or have access to any payment information. Purchases are governed by Apple's Terms of Service.

Data Storage

SplitRight stores only your app preferences locally on your device — including your selected currency and any in-app purchases you have made. This data never leaves your device.

Children's Privacy

SplitRight does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app is rated 4+ and contains no content directed at children.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
